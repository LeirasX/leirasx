ABOUT ME

Hey, I'm Francisco. Most people call me LX.


I don't do fluff. I'm obsessed with frameworks that actually change behavior. I spent years studying why we do what we do, and more importantly, why we don't do what we say we will.


My work is influenced by the directness of Mark Manson, the simplicity of Derek Sivers, and the precision of Apple's design philosophy. I believe in high-contrast living: work hard, rest deeply. Speak truth, or stay silent.
